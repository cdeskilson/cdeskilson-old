CD Eskilson grew up in Southern California and is a queer writer, educator, and editor. They currently live near Los Angeles where they are an associate editor for the Exposition Review.

Their work has appeared or is forthcoming in Teen Vogue, the Cardiff Review, After the Pause, and elsewhere. They like reenacting David Lynch movies and drinking coffee.
