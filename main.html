CD Eskilson grew up in Southern California and is a queer writer, educator, and editor. They currently live near Los Angeles where they edit poetry for the Exposition Review.

Their work has appeared in Teen Vogue, After the Pause, Poetry Quarterly, and elsewhere. They like reenacting David Lynch movies and drinking coffee.
